Paper Submission

Read the following instructions.

Paper Submission Guideline
  • Authors should submit their original unpublished work in PDF format via easy chair account.
  • If any author does not have easy chair account. First he/she needs to create his account on easy chair (easychair.org) and then follow the link given for “Online Paper Submission”.
  • Simultaneous submissions (papers already submitted to other conferences/journals) are not allowed.
  • Submission should strictly follow recommended IEEE conference template.
  • The decision regarding the acceptance of paper is at the discretion of Technical Program Committee
  • Paper Length: Up to 5 pages, including figures, tables & references. A maximum of two additional pages are permitted, with an additional charge of INR 500 per page, to be paid during author registration.
Review Process: Blind review process of peers will be As follows:
  • Papers will be accepted or rejected based on at least two independent reviewers.
  • Maximum 5 papers will be assigned to a single reviewer
  • Each reviewer will be given maximum 5 no. of papers to review in minimum of one month time
  • The number of submissions will determine the number of reviewers required
  • Plagiarism checking Committee initially checks the plagiarism in the submitted paper by plagiarism checking tools.
Submission Format and Link
